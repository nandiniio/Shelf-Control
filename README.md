# 📚✨ Shelf Control

A reading tracker for people who romanticize their lives, buy books faster than they finish them, and swear they'll read *just one more chapter*.
Shelf Control helps you keep track of your reading goals, discover your next favorite book, and turn your bookshelf into something a little more magical.

🌙 Dark mode included, of course.

---

## 🌷 What you can do

📖 Track your reading progress

🎯 Set annual reading goals and actually watch yourself get closer to them

🪄 Get personalized book recommendations based on your favorite genres

📊 Explore reading analytics and insights

🌈 Customize your experience with a colorful, reader-friendly interface

🌙 Switch between light and dark mode depending on your current main-character mood

## 💌 Why I Made This

Most reading trackers feel either too serious or too cluttered. I wanted something that felt cozy, colorful, and fun to use - a place where tracking your reading feels just as enjoyable as discovering your next favorite book.
