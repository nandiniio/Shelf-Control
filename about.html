About Shelf Control

Shelf Control is a personal reading tracker that helps users organise their reading journey. Users can add books, track their progress, write reviews, and organise books into different lists like Completed, TBR, and In-progress.

Features

  • Add and manage books
  • Track reading status
  • User login and signup
  • Edit and delete books
